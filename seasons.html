Season 2025–2026

How to Start Your Startup Event

Organized on February 16, 2026, the "How to Start Your Startup" event was successfully held with the participation of more than 250 students, exceeding 300+ registrants overall. The event promoted entrepreneurship and innovation by connecting students with experienced professionals from various industries.

Through insightful sessions, speakers shared expertise in startup development, business growth, leadership, communication, and personal branding. The event provided attendees with practical knowledge, real-world experiences, and valuable networking opportunities — inspiring them to explore entrepreneurial pathways and develop their own business ideas.

Regional Event

Hosted at Ahram Canadian University, the Regional Event served as a premier platform for innovation, entrepreneurship, and youth empowerment. The event brought together students, entrepreneurs, industry professionals, and academic leaders in an inspiring environment focused on knowledge exchange.

A key highlight was hosting the semi-finals of the Rally Entrepreneurs Competition, featuring delegations from Badr University in Assiut, 6th of October University, 6th of October Technological University, Minia University, Beni Suef University, and the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport — Smart Village Branch.

Universities Football League

Organized in collaboration with the Rally Society at October 6 Technological University, the tournament brought together 18 teams representing three major universities: 6 teams each from Al-Ahram Canadian University, October 6 Technological University, and October 6 University.

The tournament aimed to strengthen student engagement, encourage teamwork, and foster healthy competition through sports. Held at Captain Football Fields, the league attracted massive audience support — proudly sponsored by Curva, CR7, and Red Bull. The championship match was highlighted by professional footballers Ahmed El Shimi (Wadi Degla FC) and Mohamed Hamdi (Zamalek SC).

Best Members & Board Honoring Ceremony

Rally ACU celebrated the achievements of its outstanding members and board in a dedicated honoring ceremony — recognizing those who went above and beyond throughout the 2025–2026 season. Rally Team Ramadan Iftar Day

To celebrate the holy month of Ramadan and strengthen team spirit, Rally ACU organized a warm Ramadan Iftar gathering for all team members. The day provided an opportunity for the team to come together in a welcoming and friendly atmosphere, sharing values of unity, collaboration, and community.

The gathering brought together members from all committees — Marketing, Business Development, Operations, Entrepreneurs, and Talent Management — creating a space for meaningful conversations, networking, and relationship building beyond regular organizational tasks.
