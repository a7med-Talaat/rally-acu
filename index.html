Our Story

Who We Are

Rally Ahram Canadian University is a student organization committed to developing future leaders, entrepreneurs, and innovators by creating opportunities that extend beyond the classroom. Through entrepreneurship events, competitions, workshops, networking sessions, sports activities, and community initiatives, we provide students with practical experiences that enhance both their personal and professional growth.

Our goal is to build a dynamic community where students can develop essential skills, connect with industry professionals, exchange ideas, and transform their potential into meaningful impact. By fostering collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning, Rally empowers students to become active contributors to their communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers to help you understand what Rally ACU is all about and how you can fit in.

Any student currently enrolled at Ahram Canadian University can join, regardless of their faculty, major, or year of study. We believe that innovation and leadership thrive best when minds from diverse academic backgrounds collaborate.

Not at all! One of the core missions of Rally ACU is to develop your skills. We provide full onboarding, training workshops, and peer mentorship across all specialized committees to help you learn marketing, business development, operations, and leadership from the ground up.

We have five active committees that you can join: Marketing (design, content, social media), Entrepreneurs (mentorship, workshops), Operations (event logistics, organization), Business Development (sponsorship, partnerships), and Talent Management (human resources, team tracking). You can select your preference in the form!

The process is simple: click "Fill Registration Form" below, submit your details, and our Talent Management team will review your application. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a friendly offline or online interview to finalize selection.
